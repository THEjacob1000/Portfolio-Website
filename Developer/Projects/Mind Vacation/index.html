About Me

Meet Milly Vanzwol, the creative powerhouse behind Mind Vacation. Often described as a 'crazy little creative fairy,' Milly is passionately devoted to unlocking the power of imagination. With a paintbrush in one hand and a dream in the other, she crafts intricate coloring books and stunning art prints that serve as portals to another world—a world where female empowerment and the majesty of Mother Nature reign supreme. Milly believes that everyone deserves a break from the mundane, a chance to escape the stresses of daily life. Through her work, she invites you to take a mental sojourn, to step into a realm of limitless creativity and serenity. Come discover the transformative experience that is Mind Vacation, all lovingly brought to life by Milly's boundless imagination.
